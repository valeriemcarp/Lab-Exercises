Sebastian Fan Site!

I have created this site in loving devotion to my friend Bella's dog, Sebastian!

Sebastian is a 1 1/2 year old French Mastiff. His favorite things include rolling down the car window, playing with his squeaky green ball, and lying on his back.

Here I've included some fun Sebastian Stats, some of my favorite pictures of him, and more!

Sebastian Stats

Let's look at some fun facts about Sebastian, and other French Mastiffs:

  • French Mastiffs are actually formally called Dogue de Bordeaux.
  • French Mastiffs have a lifespan of 5-8 years.
  • They are known to drool - a lot! This is because of their face's shape and folds.
  • Sebastian can't keep food in his mouth for long! His jowls get in the way and he takes long to eat.
  • But he's still very smart! Sebastian can open the backyard door.
  • Sebastian loves to pose! He even has an instagram you can follow below.
  • But most of all, he's super silly. Sebastian makes all kinds of faces and poses. And he can only run diagonally!
